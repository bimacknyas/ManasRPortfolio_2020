about me

I am a Full Stack designer from Bangalore, India. I build awesome things for Mobile and Web by sketching, Crafting and ideating the experience for user. I work across Information Architecture, User Research, High level Experience Design and Interactive prototyping to craft product that are incredibly easy and delightful to use.


As a designer and developer, I understand the perfect user interface should look good and work even better. I always think from a user’s perspective, because I believe a product is meaningful only when people truly love it.
