Design Philosophy
Design is the reduction of complexity into calm — not the decoration of confusion.
  • 01
    Outcomes over Outputs I measure success by user behavior change and business impact — not by deliverables shipped or screens designed.
  • 02
    Research First, Always Every design decision is a hypothesis. Observation, not assumption, is the only valid source of truth.
  • 03
    Systems Over Screens Great products emerge from coherent systems — not collections of beautiful but disconnected screens.
  • 04
    AI as Collaborator I design AI not as a feature, but as a thinking partner — making its reasoning visible, its limits honest, its help genuine.
